FAQs

Questions candidates ask before enrolling.

Who is this program for?
Freshers, final-year students, CA and CFA candidates, working professionals, tech enthusiasts, business graduates, and anyone building a structured path into management consulting and consulting-aligned roles.
Is this program self-paced?
Core content — drills, the case study library, and recorded sessions — is self-paced. Live sessions, 1:1 mentorship, and mock interviews run on a fixed cohort schedule.
Do I need a consulting background?
No. The curriculum starts from consulting fundamentals in Phase 0 and builds up, with no prior case experience assumed.
What if I come from a non-commerce background?
The program is built for varied backgrounds — engineering, sciences, humanities, and more. Frameworks and cases are taught from first principles, and mentorship is matched to your profile.
What makes this different from other consulting prep programs?
A structured curriculum, mentor-led 1:1 feedback, real practice volume (50+ cases, 200+ drills), and placement support — built around the Indian consulting market specifically.
How does placement support work?
In-house HR consulting reviews your profile, helps target the right firms and roles for your background, and shares relevant job leads as you near interview readiness.
Are there live sessions?
Yes — 10 live mentor-led sessions, plus 1:1 case study calls and mock interviews.
Will I get a certificate?
Yes. You'll receive a certificate of completion and a shareable LinkedIn credential.
What tools and drills are included?
Calculation, structure, chart, subjective, and guesstimate drills, a 50+ case study library, AI mock interview access, and the consulting.io practice tool.
How does pricing work?
Pricing depends on your background and the active cohort. Share your details above and admissions will share your personalised fee structure.
Is there mentor support?
Yes — direct access to mentors for 1:1 calls, case feedback, mock interviews, and resume reviews.
What results can I expect?
Expect a structured improvement in your case structuring, speed, and interview readiness, backed by practice volume and mentor feedback most self-study prep can't match. Outcomes also depend on your starting point and effort.
